## 摘要
质谱分析中的开放修饰搜索（OMS）是一种数据密集型工作负载，其性能主要受限于参考数据的移动而非计算。先前的OMS加速器大多在隔离环境中进行评估，导致难以理解跨平台的系统级权衡。本文首次通过不仅研究通用平台，还研究新兴的以内存和存储为中心的架构，包括GPU、近存储FPGA、DRAM近内存处理、ReRAM/PCM内存处理以及3D NAND/FeNAND存储内处理，在一致的算法和准确性假设下，进行基于工作负载驱动的跨平台质谱搜索加速器调查。利用基于二进制超维度计算（HDC）的OMS公式，将相似性评估简化为轻量级位操作原语，并容忍设备级非理想性，我们在内存中心架构上实现了稳健执行，尽管存在设备级非理想性和有限的计算能力。总体而言，本研究将内存和存储中心架构视为大规模、高速搜索加速的关键架构突破，实现了高达>100倍的加速和>40,000倍的能效提升。

## Abstract
Open modification search (OMS) in mass spectrometry (MS) is a data-intensive workload whose performance is dominantly limited by reference data movement rather than computation. Prior OMS accelerators have largely been evaluated in isolation, making it difficult to understand system-level trade-offs across platforms. This paper presents the first workload-driven, cross-platform survey of accelerators for MS search by studying not only commodity platforms, but also emerging memory- and storage-centric architectures, including GPUs, near-storage FPGAs, DRAM near-memory processing, ReRAM/PCM in-memory processing, and 3D NAND/FeNAND in-storage processing, under consistent algorithmic and accuracy assumptions. Leveraging a binary hyperdimensional computing (HDC)-based OMS formulation that reduces similarity evaluation to lightweight bitwise primitives and tolerates device-level non-idealities, we enable a robust execution on memory-centric architectures despite device-level non-idealities and limited computing capability. Overall, this study identifies memory- and storage-centric architectures as a key architectural breakthrough for large-scale, high-speed search acceleration, delivering up to >100x speedup and >40,000x improvement in energy efficiency.
